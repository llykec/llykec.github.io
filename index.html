Services
Our mission: To eliminate language barriers in healthcare settings by providing trained volunteer interpreters to health care & nonprofit organizations and facilities that serve the underserved LEP communities in the Bay Area.
  • Languages
  • Cantonese
  • Mandarin
  • Spanish
  • Korean
  • Vietnamese
  • Tagalog
  • Interpretation
  • We provide interpretation services for patients and providers at healthcare events or settings such as health fairs, clinical appointments, workshops, medical consultations and professional panels.
  • Translation
  • We translate documents for healthcare organizations such as consent forms, educational material, brochures, presentations, as well as transcriptions of clinical appointments.
Interested in collaborating with us? We are continually looking to collaborate with new partnerships! Let us how we can support your healthcare organization connect with your constituents.
